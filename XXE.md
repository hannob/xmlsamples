Many XML APIs and libraries were historically to be vulnerable to XXE by default. Over time, most
have fixed those vulnerabilties and have adopted secure defaults.

* Python: [CVE-2013-1665](https://www.cve.org/CVERecord?id=CVE-2013-1665)
* .NET: [CVE-2016-3255](https://www.cve.org/CVERecord?id=CVE-2016-3255)
* libxml2: [CVE-2013-0339](https://www.cve.org/CVERecord?id=CVE-2013-0339)
